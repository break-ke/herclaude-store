About herclaude

Bridging ancient Daoist wisdom with the modern world.

Our Story

herclaude was born from a deep reverence for the Daoist talismanic tradition — a living art form that has been passed down through generations of lineage holders. We believe that these sacred charms are not mere artifacts but active spiritual tools that can bring protection, prosperity, peace, and love to those who use them with sincere intention.

Every talisman at herclaude is hand-crafted by practitioners who have studied under established masters. We use traditional materials — hand-dyed rice papers, mineral cinnabar inks, and gold dust — and each piece is consecrated through authentic rituals that have been preserved for centuries.

Our mission is to make these authentic, consecrated talismans accessible to people around the world while honoring the traditions from which they come. We work directly with Daoist practitioners, support their communities, and ensure that every talisman leaves our hands with the same reverence with which it was created.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these talismans authentic?

Yes. Each talisman is hand-written by a Daoist practitioner who has trained in their lineage's tradition. We work with practitioners from the Maoshan, Longmen, and Celestial Masters lineages. Every talisman is consecrated through traditional rituals including opening light (开光 — Kāi Guāng).

How long does shipping take?

Orders are prepared within 1–3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5–10 business days within the US, and 10–20 business days internationally. Express shipping is available at checkout.

Can I return a talisman?

Due to the consecrated nature of our talismans, we cannot accept returns for spiritual items once they have left our facility. If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours and we will replace it.

How should I care for my talisman?

Keep your talisman in a clean, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid placing it in bathrooms or on the floor. If the paper becomes worn, you may carefully transfer it to a new red pouch. We recommend replacing talismans annually during the Lunar New Year.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes that may apply in their country.

Can I request a specific consecration intention?

Absolutely. You can include your intention in the order notes during checkout, and our practitioners will incorporate it into the consecration ritual.
